Free Online Sign Language Classes

by Diosa Temblor

Good news! Online sign language classes are now available for free! Thanks to the Idaho Foundation for the Deaf, everyone has a chance to learn American Sign Language (ASL) without a cost for 3 months. This is offered with a fee by certain public and private organizations in Idaho and the said foundation took the initiative to give more people access to learning the language for free for a limited time. This initiative is expected to result in more people getting interested in learning ASL, thus, helping our deaf and hard of hearing community.

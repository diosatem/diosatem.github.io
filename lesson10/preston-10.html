Festival of Lights

by Joy T.

Thanksgiving is near but, who is excited for the annual "Idaho Festival of Lights" which happens right after Thanksgiving? I'm sure everybody is! Who wouldn't want a lighted parade, fireworks, and festive displays in the city park? If you want to feel the spirit of Christmas already, come to Preston where the main street is full of Christmas lights during the festival.

This year, although there are some adjustment of the way the festival is usually handled due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the organizers are assuring the public that they will feel the same festive spirit. They will still see a parade and Christmas display on houses and buildings all over the city. Learn more.

Experience the Cold Geyser at the Geyser Park

by Joy T.

Whether you are new in town or have been here all your life, you deserve to see this peculiar tourist attraction in the Soda Springs area.

There are a number of parks nearby, one of which is the Geyser Park. It is both natural and man-made geyser that goes off every hour on the hour! Now, you might be wondering why it's both natural and man-made. The town hit this natural water pocket while drilling for water and it flooded the whole town and since then, they capped it let it erupt hourly.

It is open for 24 hours and it's great for kids and and worth 10 minutes of your time. How to get there.
